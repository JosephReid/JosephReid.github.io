ABOUT ME

My name is Joe Reid; I am a 24-year-old student that lives video games. I have been hooked on games ever since I was 6 years old when I got my first console, the Nintendo 64. The game that started it all? The Legend of Zelda Ocarina of Time. I remember (even back then) thinking I want to make my own worlds and play through them. Today nothing has changed, I want to be a part of the minds that inspire.

I always thought I wanted to be a concept artist. It was the most glamourous of the jobs and felt like something that would leave my mark on a game. However, I was pointed towards UI/UX. In all honesty, it didn’t sound like my cup of tea. Menus are boring I thought.

With time my opinion changed. I began to see how important it is and how creative you can be with “just a menu”. Even the best games can be ruined by bad UI and I can be a part of the teams that invents new and interesting ways to give the best experience to the player.

I enjoy games, understand what makes them good and I have the ability to give something to the industry I love.
