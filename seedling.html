Platform: Web
Build: HTML5 / JavaScript

In Seedling, you play as a small fledgling seed pod, trying to grow as tall as you possibly can. This small arcade game focuses around repetitively going through cycles of planting and growing until you can’t react fast enough!

December 2015 – December 2015 / 72 Hours

This game was created for the Ludum Dare 34 game jam. The themes for this jam were ‘Growth’ and ‘Two Button Controls’. At this point in time, I wanted to develop my programming skills outside of an engine, so I focused on my strength at the time ( JavaScript ) to build the best game I could in the time available. Time management was definitely a struggle, I don’t think I’d ever made something so fast! Especially as I had to handle all the asset production as well, so I was sure to keep the scope of the game small and relative to what I thought would be achievable. I managed it, just. I believe I submitted my entry around 3 minutes before the deadline!
